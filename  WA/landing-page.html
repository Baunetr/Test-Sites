Why ExpensePath?

Simple Set-Up

ExpensePath takes care of setup the same day free of charge. Setup usually takes 1 employee under an hour. Free trials typically run 2 to 6 weeks, to ensure comfort with a full cycle of expense reports.

Anytime / Anywhere Access

Employees and Admin login from any connected device. Free mobile apps for iPhone/iPad and Android. Automated workflow from submitters to managers to Finance.

Easily Create Expense Reports

Dropdown menus for expense categories, currencies and project codes. Capture receipt images by mobile app, email them to us or upload from PC. Automatic currency conversion based on date of transaction.

Easily Review Expense Reports

Policy violations highlighted for quick review. Expenses re-ordered chronologically with receipt images tied directly to expense details (no more envelope of receipts). Edit minor mistakes and approve.

Automate Policy & Compliance

Finance defines allowable policies including spending limits. Policy violations highlighted for submitters and reviewers in real-time. Employees submit on time and within the rules.

Accounting Integrations

Export expense data directly to accounting system, eliminating manual re-keying. Full mapping of all necessary data to company configuration for tracking and financial reporting.

Unparalleled Support

ExpensePath provides ongoing support to all users and Finance departments. Response times are within a few minutes from experts who understand our product and our customers' needs.

Affordable Pricing
